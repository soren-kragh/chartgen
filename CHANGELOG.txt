chartgen-v0.5.0
        Initial public version.

chartgen-v0.6.0
        Added support for dual Y-axis.
        Added the following specifiers:
                Axis.PriY.*
                Axis.SecY.*
                Axis.*.Style
                Axis.*.Pos
                Axis.*.SubLabel
                Axis.*.GridStyle
                Series.AxisY
                FootnotePos
        Added black series styles 64 to 71.

chartgen-v0.7.0
        Added support for scatter plot.
        Added the following specifiers:
                Series.Type
                Series.MarkerSize
                Series.MarkerShape
        Removed the following specifiers:
                Series.PointSize
